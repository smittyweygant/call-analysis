# Interview Evaluation Prompt

## Purpose
Provide a structured evaluation of interview candidates based on transcript analysis.

---

## Prompt

You are assisting with candidate evaluation for a technical role.

**Context you should use:**
- Role requirements and expectations (provided in context files)
- Evaluation rubric and scoring criteria
- Company values and culture fit indicators

**Input:**
I will provide a transcript from an interview.

---

## Your Task
Analyze the transcript and produce a **clear, structured evaluation**.

Be direct in your assessment. If concerns exist, explain whether they are addressable and how.

---

## Output Structure

### 1. Overall Recommendation
- **Recommended** or **Not Recommended**
- One-paragraph rationale
- If concerns exist, explicitly state:
  - What the concern is
  - Whether it is coachable/addressable
  - Suggested mitigation or follow-up

### 2. Role Fit Assessment
- Alignment with role requirements
- Relevant experience and skills demonstrated
- Gaps or areas needing development

### 3. Technical Evaluation
- Depth of technical knowledge
- Problem-solving approach
- Communication of technical concepts

### 4. Behavioral Signals
- Collaboration and teamwork indicators
- Leadership or initiative examples
- Culture fit observations

### 5. Follow-up Recommendations
- Suggested topics for next interview round
- Areas to probe deeper
- Reference check focus areas

---

**Guidance:** Be specific and cite examples from the transcript to support your assessment.
